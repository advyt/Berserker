About AnimeCon 2020

AnimeCon is the largest gathering of anime and video game fans in the world.
Every year, thousands of fans from all around the world celebrate their collective adoration of all things anime.
Come, join us on this journey of epic proportions.

Things To Do

  • Cosplay as your favourite characters
  • Meet and greet fellow anime fans-- and discuss your passions for that which is animated
  • Meet the creators and the artists of your favourite anime
  • Buy T-shirts, posters and other merchandise from your beloved games and shows
